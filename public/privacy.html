Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, We Make Reality uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. We Make Reality uses cookies to help We Make Reality identify and track visitors, their usage of http://www.wemakereality.com, and their website access preferences. We Make Reality visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using We Make Reality's websites, with the drawback that certain features of We Make Reality's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to We Make Reality's use of cookies.
